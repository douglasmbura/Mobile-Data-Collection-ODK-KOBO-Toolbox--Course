ABOUT THE COURSE

Our course is built around the Open Data Kit (ODK) platform, which is widely considered to be the most user-friendly and flexible data collection tool available. With ODK, you can easily create, deploy, and manage mobile forms for data collection. 

We will take you through the process step by step, from creating your first form to deploying it in the field.The course is divided into five modules, each one building on the previous one. The modules will cover topics such as:

1. Creating a form 
2. Deploying the form 
3. Collecting data 
4. Managing data 
5. Visualizing data

Each module will be accompanied by a series of hands-on exercises, so you can put your new skills into practice right away. We will also provide you with a detailed guide to troubleshoot common issues that you may encounter.

Our course is designed to be simple and straightforward, with no unnecessary balderdash or theory. We believe that the best way to learn is by doing, and that's why our course is focused on hands-on exercises and real-world examples.

So, if you're looking for a straightforward, easy-to-use solution for mobile data collection, our Mobile Data Collection with ODK course is the perfect choice. Sign up today and get started with your mobile data collection in just about 3 of hours.
